A **PID Controller** continuously calculates an error value and applies corrections using three distinct gains:

  • P (Proportional Gain): Reacts to the present error.
  • I (Integral Gain): Accumulates past errors to remove steady-state error.
  • D (Derivative Gain): Predicts future errors and smooths response.
